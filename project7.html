Animated Landscape

Tool(s): Adobe Animate, Adobe Stock, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Firefly

Project Description: A short 10-second animation of a modern coastal city at sunset. I used Adobe Stock and Photoshop to create the moving parts in the animations, like the lights and plane. I used Firefly to generate the background, and stitched it all together in Animate with the timeline tool.
